Las Vegas: Mega‑Entertainment

Las Vegas has always billed itself as the world entertainment capital, and today it seems determined to prove it. From global pop residencies and major sports events to tech conventions, there is always a reason for the Strip to pulse with energy. The skyline is now dominated by the luminous Sphere — a venue that can transform into anything from a glittering planet to a swirling ocean and serves as a billboard for the city and its larger–than–life ambitions. Wander a few blocks and you will find mega resorts, luxury shopping, Michelin starred restaurants and, of course, casinos buzzing day and night. Beyond the tables and slots, Vegas nightlife ranges from EDM superclubs to intimate speakeasies and live shows that rotate with each season. The city does have a downside: prices. Resort fees, show tickets and even cocktails have climbed in recent years, making a weekend trip far more expensive than it used to be. Still, if your budget allows, few places offer such a kaleidoscope of spectacle in one glittering package.
